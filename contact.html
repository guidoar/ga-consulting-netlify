Common questions

How long does a project take?

Most projects take 1 to 3 weeks depending on scope. A focused CRM build can be done in a week. A full company setup with multiple databases and automations takes closer to 3 weeks.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, fully remote. I work with clients across Europe and beyond. All calls are via Google Meet or Zoom, and all communication is async-friendly.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope. I share a clear fixed-price proposal after the discovery call — no hourly surprises, no hidden fees. You always know exactly what you're paying before we start.

Do I need a Notion account already?

No. I can help you set one up from scratch. Notion has a free plan for individuals and affordable team plans. I'll recommend the right one for your situation.

What happens after delivery?

Every project includes a walkthrough session, a Loom video guide, and a written handoff document. You'll also get 7 days of support after delivery for any questions.

Can you train my whole team?

Yes. Team training can be added to any project. I'll run a live session with your team to walk through the system and answer questions in real time.
